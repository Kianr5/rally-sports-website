Introducing RallyFest

More than a tournament.

RallyFest is a festival-style 3v3 soccer tournament that combines competitive play with music, food, entertainment, and community.

The goal is to create an annual tradition that players look forward to every year.

  • Two-day tournament
  • Festival atmosphere
  • Music and food vendors
  • Sponsor activations
  • Championship matches
