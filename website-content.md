# Company Information

## Company Name

Rally Sports

## Description

Rally Sports is a sports entertainment company that develops, owns,
operates, and scales sports properties. Through world-class competitions
and festival-style experiences, Rally Sports creates annual traditions
that bring communities together while building enduring consumer brands.

## Mission

Bring communities together through unforgettable sporting experiences
centered around competition, culture, and connection.

## Vision

Become the world's largest and most celebrated sporting events company
by creating iconic annual sports festivals that communities anticipate
every year.

## Slogan

Competition brings us together. Community brings us back.
